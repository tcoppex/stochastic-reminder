Manage reminders with customized notification randomly during the week.

<b>Features</b>

* Schedule fixed and random reminders up to 48H ranges,
* Triggered on specific days of the week,
* Specify both the title and long-form multiline message,
* Group same reminder notifications together to avoid clutting,
* Specify the alarm type: sound, vibration, both or muted,
* Use exact alarm for non muted notifications.
* Use battery preserving scheduling on muted notifications.
